Dal tadka

Dal Tadka is a popular Indian dish made with lentils and a flavorful tempering of spices. Here’s a simple and delicious recipe to make Dal Tadka at home.


Ingredients:-

For Dal
2 tbsp ghee / घी
½ cup tuvar dal, soaked / तुवर दाल
3 tbsp moong dal, soaked / मसूर दाल
1 inch ginger, sliced / अदरक
Salt to taste / नमक स्वाद अनुसार
¼ tsp turmeric powder / हल्दी पाउडर
1 tsp red chilli powder / लाल मिर्च पाउडर
1 green chilli / हरी मिर्च
1 ½ cups water / पानी

For Tempering
1 tbsp ghee / घी
1 tbsp oil / तेल
½ tsp cumin seeds / ज़ीरा
1 inch ginger, finely chopped / अदरक
½ tbsp garlic, finely chopped / लहसुन
1 medium onion, finely chopped / प्याज़
1 tsp red chilli powder / लाल मिर्च पाउडर
¼ tsp turmeric powder / हल्दी पाउडर
Salt to taste / नमक स्वाद अनुसार

For 2nd Tempering
2 tbsp ghee / घी
2 tbsp oil / तेल
3-4 garlic cloves, sliced / लहसुन
2-3 whole dry kashmiri red chillies / खड़ी कश्मीरी लाल मिर्च
A pinch of asafoetida / हींग
½ tsp kashmiri red chilli powder / कश्मीरी लाल मिर्च पाउडर
Coriander leaves, for garnish / धनिया


Instructions:-

  1. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker, add tuvar dal and moong dal to the pressure cooker and roast for 2-3 minutes.
  2. Add in ginger, salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt and green chilli. Mix well.
  3. Add water, cover and allow to cook for 3 whistles.
  4. Meanwhile, heat ghee in a pan, add cumin seeds, ginger and garlic, saute till fragrant.
  5. Add onions and saute till translucent. Add chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt, mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes, till the rawness of the spices is gone.
  6. Add the boiled dal to this tempering and allow to cook for simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, prepare the second tempering.
  8. Heat ghee in a pan, add some water. Add garlic cloves, whole dry kashmiri red chillies, asafoetida, kashmiri red chilli powder and mix well.
  9. Cook till it splutters.
  10. Pour the prepared dal into a serving dish, garnish with coriander leaves.
  11. Pour the prepared tempering and serve hot.
